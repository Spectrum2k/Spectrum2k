## About me

I'm a DevOps / SRE engineer from Moscow, focused on infrastructure, reliability, automation, monitoring, self-hosted services, and developer experience.

I work with production systems, CI/CD, observability, Linux servers, containerized workloads, virtualization, and internal platforms. Alongside my main engineering work, I also build hobby projects, experiment with new technologies, and help beginners understand programming, infrastructure, and modern development practices.

I'm also involved in **AltHub Project**, where I work with a small team on internal services, community projects, infrastructure, experiments, and long-term technical initiatives.

---

## What I do

- Build and maintain Linux-based infrastructure
- Design CI/CD pipelines and deployment workflows
- Work with monitoring, alerting, logs, and incident diagnostics
- Operate self-hosted services and internal platforms
- Automate repetitive engineering tasks
- Review code, architecture, infrastructure changes, and project decisions
- Experiment with web, backend, mobile, and game-related technologies
- Help communities and teams organize technical work more clearly
